Why 90-Minute Sleep Cycles Matter

March 15, 2025 | 8 min read

Understanding Sleep Architecture

Human sleep consists of repeating cycles composed of four distinct stages:

Stage 1: Light Sleep (N1)

  • Duration: 5-10 minutes
  • Transition between wakefulness and sleep
  • Easily awakened

Stage 2: True Sleep Onset (N2)

  • Duration: 20-30 minutes
  • Body temperature drops
  • Heart rate slows
"The 90-minute cycle represents the optimal duration for completing all critical sleep phases."
- National Sleep Foundation
